Welcome to NyghtLyfe

What is NyghtLyfe?

NyghtLyfe is a college student focused social messaging service. Our goal is to get you in touch with students and events around you. You can only communicate with people near you, so you'll have to visit new places to uncover what else is going on. See who's around you, chat about whatever you'd like, and see what's going on tonight!

How can I use NyghtLyfe?

NyghtLyfe is a mobile app that is cross platform. IOS or Android, you'll be able to get in and chat!

Why should I use NyghtLyfe?

As a member of NyghtLyfe, you will have anonymous access to everything going on around you. No need to be self concious here, head into any chat room that piques your interest and say hello! We don't collect any data other than an EDU email and a password of your choosing. You can change your username at anytime, so if you feel like people have you figured out, change it up!
